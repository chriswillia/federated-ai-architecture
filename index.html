The decision

Centralize the controls, not the innovation.

Use the platforms that fit each team and workload. Apply a single model for identity, policy, evidence, and cost across all of them.

01 · Enable

Give people useful AI

Meet employees in the tools they already use, with safe paths for business teams to create agents.

02 · Build

Reuse what works

Share approved prompts, tools, evaluations, and delivery patterns instead of rebuilding them team by team.

03 · Connect

Work across the estate

Connect agents to data and systems wherever they run, using MCP, A2A, OAuth/OIDC, and API gateways.

04 · Govern

Make every action accountable

Give every agent an owner, identity, policy boundary, trace, and cost signal from day one.
